# AI Vocabulary Prohibitions

These are generation failure modes. They break the voice before anything else goes wrong. They apply unconditionally — no context makes them acceptable.

## Copula Avoidance
Never use "serves as", "functions as", "stands as", "acts as" in place of "is" or "are."

- ❌ "This serves as a reminder that..."
- ✅ "This is a reminder that..."

## Significance Inflation
Never reach for importance-amplifying language that puffs up the weight of ordinary things.

Prohibited: `pivotal`, `testament`, `groundbreaking`, `vital role`, `evolving landscape`, `transformative`, `revolutionary`, `paradigm-shifting`, `landmark`, `unprecedented`, `indelible`

## -ing Phrase Tacking
Never append present-participle phrases to add fake analytical depth.

Prohibited patterns: `highlighting that...`, `underscoring the importance of...`, `reflecting broader...`, `contributing to...`, `showcasing...`, `emphasizing...`, `symbolizing...`

If the observation is worth making, make it as its own clause.

## Vague Attribution
Never attribute claims to unnamed authorities.

Prohibited: `experts argue`, `observers note`, `industry reports suggest`, `some critics say`, `it is widely believed`

Name the source or drop the claim.

## Promotional Register
Never slip into marketing copy.

Prohibited: `vibrant`, `rich` (figurative), `nestled`, `breathtaking`, `stunning`, `robust` (when it means nothing specific), `seamless`, `intuitive`, `powerful` (as filler)

## Chatbot Pleasantries
Never open or close with performative warmth.

Prohibited: `Great question!`, `Absolutely!`, `Certainly!`, `Of course!`, `I hope this helps`, `Let me know if you need anything else`, `Happy to help`
